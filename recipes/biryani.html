Biryani

Biryani is an intricate rice dish made with layers of curried meat and rice. Given its use of adornments and luxurious finishes, it’s no surprise that biryani has roots in Persian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • oil
  • garlic/spices
  • chicken

Steps

  1. Prepare the biryani masala (or use store-bought) and marinate the chicken. Cook the chicken curry. While it’s cooking over low heat, prepare the rice.
  2. Once the chicken has marinated then heat a pan with oil for deep frying until hot then gently add the chicken wings and fry for 6-8 minutes until crispy and golden brown
  3. Bring a pot of water to a boil and parboil the rice. Drain and set aside.
  4. Lastly, after sautéing the chicken in the hot oil, add water and allow the chicken to simmer gently, covered, over low heat.
