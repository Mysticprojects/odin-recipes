Chicken wings

This spicy chicken wings recipe is so delicious you'll be preparing it over and over again just like I do.

A beautiful platter of spicy wings with rice and fries is my absolute favourite.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • eggs
  • corn flour
  • garlic

Steps

  1. In a large bowl add the marinade ingredients to the chicken wings then mix until all the wings are evenly coated
  2. Once the chicken has marinated then heat a pan with oil for deep frying until hot then gently add the chicken wings and fry for 6-8 minutes until crispy and golden brown
  3. Once all the chicken wings have been fried set aside, as you prepare the sauce.
  4. Add the chilli sauce and tomato ketchup then cook for a further 1-2 minute
